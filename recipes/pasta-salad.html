Description

This Italian pasta salad that's loaded with tri-color rotini pasta, salami, bell peppers, and fresh mozzarella balls is quick to make and full of flavor for a delicious summertime salad.

This delicious Italian pasta salad comes together quickly with salami, fresh mozzarella, colorful bell pepper, and red onion, all tossed in Italian salad dressing.

Ingredients

  • Tri-color rotini pasta
  • Finely sliced Italian salami
  • Green and red bell peppers
  • 1 red onion
  • Canned black olives
  • Small Mozzarella balls
  • Shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup Italian-style dressing
  • 3 package Italian-style dry dressing mix

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until tender-yet-firm.
  2. Combine the pasta, salami, veggies, and mozzarella in a bowl.
  3. Toss in salad dressing, then stir in the dressing mix.
  4. Sprinkle with Parmesan before serving.
